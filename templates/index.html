AI Product Builder

Turn a raw startup idea into a development-ready SRS

Your concept is not only described, it is transformed into market context, clear differentiation logic, and build-ready product requirements.

How the engine works

What happens at each step

Step 01

Idea Input + Feature Context

The user submits a raw idea and current features. The system normalizes the input, removes unnecessary noise, and prepares an analysis-ready context.

Step 02

Market Analysis Logic

The LLM identifies real competitors, relevant URLs, overlap points, similarity scores, and a market summary. That output is then converted into structured JSON for decision-friendly analysis.

Step 03

Improvement Intelligence

Based on competitor gaps and market opportunity signals, the system suggests high-impact improvements. The user selects which strategies should be included in the final plan.

Step 04

SRS Generation + Export

Selected features are converted into an architecture-level SRS document. The final output is previewed and exported as a .docx file for implementation handoff.

From idea uncertainty to execution clarity, one guided workflow.
